Step 3: Get Coordinates from Google Maps

  1. Go to Google Maps
  2. Search for your location (e.g., "Venice, Italy")
  3. Right-click on the location on the map
  4. Click the coordinates that appear at the top of the menu
  5. The coordinates are copied! (Format: 45.4408, 12.3155)
  6. First number = lat (latitude)
  7. Second number = lng (longitude)

üîß Troubleshooting

Maps still show old data:

  • Clear browser cache (Ctrl + Shift + Delete)
  • Wait 5-10 minutes for Google's cache to refresh
  • Make sure "Automatically republish" is enabled in Google Sheets

Console shows errors:

  • Verify your Google Sheets URLs are correctly pasted
  • Check that sheets are published to the web
  • Confirm column names match exactly (case-sensitive)

Coordinates not displaying correctly:

  • Make sure lat/lng are plain numbers (no degree symbols)
  • Use decimal format: 45.4408, not 45¬∞ 26' 27"
  • Check for typos in coordinate values
